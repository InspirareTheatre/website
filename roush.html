About Kelly Hilliard Roush, Chernobyl's Fire Director...

Kelly Hilliard Roush has been a professional theatre artist for over 15 years.

She graduated from Louisiana State University with her Master of Fine Arts in Professional Actor Training and has performed in classic works, new plays and company created pieces.

In addition to performing, she has worked as a producer, director, writer, collaborator and teacher.

She has worked in California, Maine, Minnesota, Texas, North Carolina, and Louisiana.
